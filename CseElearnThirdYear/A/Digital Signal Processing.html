COURSE OBJECTIVE:

COURSE INSTRUCTOR:

  • Dr.K.S.Vishvaksenan,Associate Professor

  • To briefly review signals and systems
  • To learn discrete Fourier transform and its properties
  • To know the characteristics of IIR and FIR filters learn the design of infinite and
    finite impulse response filters for filtering undesired signals
  • To understand Finite word length effects

SYLLABUS:

  • Unit I : SIGNALS AND SYSTEMS
  • Unit II : FREQUENCY TRANSFORMATIONS
  • Unit III : IIR FILTER
  • Unit IV : FIR FILTER DESIGN
  • Unit V : FINITE WORD LENGTH EFFECTS IN DIGITAL FILTERS

*

COURSE OUTCOME:

  • Understand signals, systems and basics of digital signal processing.
  • Understand and apply DFT and FFT for the analysis of digital signals & systems.
  • Design and realize Butterworth and Chebyshev IIR filters using impulse invariant and Bilinear transformation techniques.
  • Design and realize FIR filters using window techniques and frequency sampling method.
  • Characterize finite Word length effect on digital filters.
